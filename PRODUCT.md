# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hyderabad land buyers screening an area before visiting a site, negotiating with a broker, or paying a token. Their primary job is to quickly understand money risk, growth potential, price context, and what must be independently verified next.

## Product Purpose

PlotDNA is buyer-side land intelligence that helps people decide whether a location deserves deeper verification. Success means a buyer can select land, run one clear check, understand the result, compare alternatives, and share a concise decision aid without mistaking PlotDNA for legal or title approval.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, direct, buyer-first. The product should feel calm and decisive while remaining transparent about partial coverage and uncertainty.

## Anti-references

Avoid crowded intelligence dashboards, generic AI-agent or SaaS styling, decorative complexity, repeated legal copy, and interfaces that bury the next buyer action under dense metrics.

## Design Principles

- Put the buyer's next decision ahead of educational or technical detail.
- Keep land selection separate from analysis so Check My Land remains meaningful.
- Make risk, confidence, and source limits explicit without repeating warnings everywhere.
- Preserve a clear escape path to start a new search from every result screen.
- Prefer concise, actionable evidence over generic definitions or speculative claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and interaction behavior. Support reduced motion, visible keyboard focus, touch-friendly controls, safe-area insets, readable layouts at 390px and 430px widths, and meaning that does not depend on color alone.
